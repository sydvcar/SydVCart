Milwaukee's Best After Effects & Premiere Pro - 2025

The innocence and excitement of new love bring to life Milwaukee's Best by The Wrecks in this fan-made music video. The mixed media approach conveys a whimsical, charmingly rough-around-the-edges narrative.

Written Between the Lines Avid Media Composer - 2026

With their identity reduced to a single life-altering decision, formerly incarcerated youth turn to creative writing as they navigate life post-incarceration and contend with the systemic injustices of the US justice system. This documentary looks in on a writing circle at InsideOUT Writers—an LA-based non-profit organization that offers creative writing classes to those in juvenile detention facilities, and works to reduce recidivism rates by providing services to alumni. Shot on Panasonic CX350.

In researching and developing this film, I went through the process of first getting to know Jimmy Wu, the current Executive Director of InsideOUT Writers and a lecturer at UCLA in the Visual and Performing Arts Education Program (VAPAE). I sat in on one of his classes at UCLA, and initially created a one-minute short to document some of his most recent writing. This process helped me understand the heart of the organization's transformative work and what I wanted to focus on moving forward.

In studying documentary, I also recorded two short exercises in class at UCLA. One, a video portrait of my classmate, to practice capturing people's likeness with available light. The other, an audio portrait of another classmate, to practice constructing a concise narrative from a much longer interview.

Selfie Portrait After Effects & Premiere Pro - 2024

Selfie Portrait examines concepts of identity and digital presentation. With this project, I wanted to investigate the format of short-form video content that has gained rising popularity on social media while simultaneously critiquing that very format and environment. My research is interested in how our relationships become warped based on how we portray ourselves online and the disconnect generated with our offline selves.

Model: Isabella Broccolo Music: Sahara by Hensonn

All Hung Up Premiere Pro - 2026

A discomforting late-night phone call urges a girl to contemplate her relationship with a past self and those she has left at a distance. This is a short film exercise on impressionist camera and editing techniques of 1920s cinema. It is inspired by films like A Page of Madness and Ménilmontant.

Filmed all in one day, all within my apartment, I relied fully on my rough storyboard to complete “in-camera editing,” rather than capturing coverage. This was a rewarding experiment with very minimal camera and lighting equipment.

Blind Date After Effects, Premiere Pro, & Photoshop - 2024

This short animated film was made for my Senior Thesis project in Digital Media at UNC Charlotte. I completed everything from writing, concept art, and storyboards to asset creation, rigging, and animation. The story is centered around Medusa and her journey to find a partner. Featuring an assortment of monsters and cryptids at a local speed-dating event, the film explores relatable challenges of relationships—particularly for feminine-presenting individuals. I wanted to incorporate a sense of playfulness and humor into this mundane but daunting activity to reduce its intimidating appearance while representing a diverse range of characters. Music: Anyone by Anna Vtipil

Exhibited in UNCC's 2024 Gold Reel Film Festival.

Animation involved preparing all assets in Photoshop in separate layers, then using Duik Bassel in After Effects to rig the characters. I wanted to stick with a 2D puppet-like appearance, both as a stylistic and time-conscious choice, without things looking overly stiff.

One Nightstand Photoshop & After Effects - 2024

One Nightstand continues my investigation of contemporary relationships coupled with technology, and how our devices impact us even at rest. With this project, I aim to create a fundamentally human narrative despite the absence of humans. Instead, the scene is centered around electronic/man-made objects within an intimate bedroom environment and the relationships that they insinuate. Cattle Battle GameMaker & Photoshop - 2024

This project was made in collaboration with Grace Bishop for my Game Design studio at UNC Charlotte. Inspired by early 2000s co-op flash games such as Fireboy and Watergirl, we wanted to explore a playful multiplayer PC experience. Play as the alien to abduct cows, or play as the farmer to defend them! Music by Elvis Herod Co-coded with Chelsea Brtis.

I designed and animated the farmer and UFO sprites, along with the various title cards for the game, and I handled most of the coding. Grace designed the background and animated the cow sprites. This was a great collaborative experience in which I learned a lot about frame-by-frame animation and preparing 2D assets for use in video games.

How to Summon a Ghost Website After Effects, Photoshop, & Dreamweaver - 2022

This mobile webpage was made as an exercise in interactive design. With my signature dark yet playful approach, the assignment involved providing viewers step-by-step instructions. I first mocked up my design in Photoshop, then animated the main visuals in After Effects, and coded everything using Adobe Dreamweaver.
