Meet Sydney Carmer

My name is Sydney Carmer (she/they), also known by my online handle SydVC, and I am a multimedia artist based in North Carolina. In May 2024 I will earn my BFA in Art with a double concentration in Digital Media and Illustration at The University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I am also in the Arts + Architecture Honors Program, and double minored in Psychology and Art History. I specialize in post-production film and motion graphics but pride myself on being a sort of jack-of-all-trades. Any creative challenge can be solved with my wide skill set. I'm an organized, hard-working quick learner who is always ready to adapt to feedback. Please don't hesitate to reach out through my business email: sydvcart@gmail.com
