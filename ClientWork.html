Freelance Projects

Lil Mabu Visualizers Premiere Pro & After Effects - 2024

For his performances in the summer of 2024, I created visualizers for musician Lil Mabu. I worked with a team of editors on his entire setlist and primarily tackled three videos myself. These included "Oppy Day," for which the client requested a karaoke-style approach, "Mathematical Disrespect," and an "Intermission" loop for the time in between songs, all of which followed the musician's brain-themed visual identity. This project saw a very short turnaround, with the visualizers being played at concerts less than a week after being assigned. Stock footage in combination with original clips was used.

High Cal Games KTC Trailer Premiere Pro - 2022

For the release of their first demo game, "Kill the Climbers," I created a trailer for indie North Carolina-based video game development company High Cal Games. KTC is a retro-style arcade game where you play as a yeti attempting to defend the peak of your mountain. I followed a retro-style aesthetic when making the trailer to match the preexisting company image. This trailer showcased the in-progress assets and mechanics for the game, available in early access on Steam as of May 2024.

Oil Pipeline Software Attack Graphics Illustrator - 2022

For this project, I assisted with a UNC Charlotte graduate student's research proposal in Computer Science. The research pertains to an adaptive game-based learning system for cybersecurity information. I was tasked with creating a set of graphics that illustrated this cybersecurity information in an oil pipeline system. The graphics needed to clearly and concisely visually communicate complex information to viewers with little background knowledge in the area. I established a variety of designs and color options for my client to choose from. With each iteration of my design, I adapted to feedback from the research team.
