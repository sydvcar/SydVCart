Concept Art

Witch Characters & Cottage Interior Photoshop - 2022

This project was made for my Digital Painting studio. The first character design features Myra, a witch girl known for her bad luck. The second character turnaround features Myra's familiar, nicknamed Poncho, who has both a corvid and a human form. The interior environment design is the witch's cottage.

Camelot Costume Design Photoshop - 2023

This project was made for my Character Design studio. Inspired by the stories of Camelot, I redesigned the character Morgan le Fay to fit a carnival/circus theme. Emphasis is placed on her clothing and prop design. To achieve this new theme, I imagined her taking on the role of a fortune teller.

The Wonderful Circus of Oz Photoshop - 2023

This project was made for my Character Design studio. Inspired by the stories of Oz, I redesigned the four main characters from The Wonderful Wizard of Oz to fit a carnival/circus theme. I imagined Dorothy as an acrobat/tightrope walker, the Cowardly Lion as a tamed circus lion, the Scarecrow as a juggling clown on stilts, and the Tin Man as a strong man.

Fantasy Environment Assets Photoshop - 2022

These projects were made for my Digital Painting studio. My organic asset features a willow tree, both by itself and placed in an environment. My more structural asset is an isometric fairy tale cottage, made for a character on a tiny scale.

The Haunted Hedge Board Game Photoshop & Mixed Media - 2022

This project was made for my Children's Media studio. It is a board game in which you and your family have gotten lost in a hedge maze and need to find your way out before getting caught by the ghosts that roam the land at night. The game design process included character, board, piece, packaging, and rule design.

Candy Curfew Board Game Photoshop & Mixed Media - 2023

This project was made for my Character Design studio. It is a board game in which you play as trick-or-treaters on Halloween night competing to get the most candy. However, players are also being chased by a Halloween spirit. The game design process included character, board, card, and rule design.

Narrative Art

What's in the Dark? Photoshop & Mixed Media - 2022

This project was made for my Children's Media studio. It is an interactive board book targeted at young readers. I utilized a "magic flashlight" in its construction, wherein I cut out flaps in each page that children could open. Behind the flaps are sheets of clear plastic, a spacer, and then black paper. There are then drawings on the clear plastic so that kids can use a white piece of paper made to look like a flashlight, slide it behind the plastic, and reveal the images.

Stretch for the Stars Photoshop - 2022

This project was made for my Children's Media studio. It involves a wrap-around cover design, four inner spreads, and accompanying merchandise proposals. Targeted at young readers, I utilized an unorthodox vertical composition for the book to emphasize the character's journey as a giraffe.

Goth Pigeon has a Crush Photoshop - 2022

This project was made for my Children's Media studio. It includes a wrap-around cover design and a single inner spread. The story follows two birds who seemingly have very different personalities but like each other nonetheless, teaching kids to be true to their feelings and to accept other people.

The Hungry Cipactli Photoshop - 2023

This project was made for my Character Design studio. It involves a hybrid animal design re-imagined for a younger audience. The Cipactli is a sea creature that is part crocodile, part fish, and part toad or frog. It is characterized by an insatiable hunger and has an extra mouth on every joint of its body.

Moirai 3D-Printed PLA, Electrical Cord, & Acrylic Paint - 2023

This project was made for my 3D Modeling & Digital Fabrication studio. In classical mythology, the three fates respectively spin, draw out, and cut the threads that represent every individual's life. My project is based on this story but takes on a modern twist, referencing our intertwining relationship with technology.
